SUMMARY OF POLICY

We do not collect your data. Ever. Any data generated by using this application is stored on your device only.

We do not track you. We do not profile you. We do not log or share personal information. Period.

We do not even know how to collect your data. We have nowhere physically to store your data (we have no servers). We would not even know what to do with your data if we got it. So what ever happens on this application on your device stays on your device.

If you would like to delete any data stored on your device you may go to the "Tracker" tab of this application and tap "Reset Counts".

If we ever do learn how to collect data, find a place to store your data, and figure out what to do with your data we will update this privacy policy and notify users of these changes which will include what data is collected, where and how it is stored, the retention policy of your data, and what we will do with your data.
